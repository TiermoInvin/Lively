# [Technique cues](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s5hluoQjtIM) [just changed the link](click link for a full video on the technique):
-     Full ROM (Touch chest to bar if possible, end in a dead-hang at the bottom)  
-     Control the eccentric (going down), don’t just drop down.  
-     Don’t worry about what your scapulae are doing,
