# Grip Strength
## Isometric
### Level 1 (Hook Grip)  
  -   2 Sets of 30 Second (Pronated, Active)  
  -   2 Sets of 30-Second (Supinated, Active)  
  -   2 Sets of 30 Second (Pronated, Bent-Arm)  
  -   2 Sets of 30-Second (Supinated, Bent-Arm)  
  -   2 Sets of 15-Second (One-Arm | Assisted Towel | L/R)  
### -   Level 2 (Claw Grip)  
  -   2 Sets of 30 Second (Pronated, Active)  
  -   2 Sets of 30-Second (Supinated, Active)  
  -   2 Sets of 30 Second (Pronated, Bent-Arm)  
  -   2 Sets of 30-Second (Supinated, Bent-Arm)  
  -   2 Sets of 15-Second (One-Arm | Assisted Towel | L/R)  
### -   Level 3 (Hook Grip)  
  -   2 Sets of 5 Reps (Ape Twist)  
  -   2 Sets of 30-Second (Side Swing)  
  -   2 Sets of 30-Second (Bent-Arm, Commando Hang)  
  -   2 Sets of 15-Second (Two-Arm, Towel Hang)  
  -   2 Sets of 5 Reps (Grip Change)  
### -   Level 4  
  -   2 Sets of 30 Second (Pronated, Front Swing)  
  -   2 Sets of 30-Second (Supinated, Front Swing)  
  -   2 Sets of 30 Second (Pronated, Side Walks)  
  -   2 Sets of 30-Second (Supinated, Bent-Arm Swings)  
  -   2 Sets of 30-Second (Claw-Grip, 1A Towel Hang)  
### -   Level 5  
  -   2 Sets of 30 Second (Pronated, 1-Arm Switches)  
  -   2 Sets of 30-Second (Minitwists)  
  -   2 Sets of 30 Second (2-Towel Swing)  
  -   2 Sets of 30-Second (2 Bent-Arm Towel Swing)  
  -   2 Sets of 30-Second (Extended Ape Twist)  
### -   Level 6  
  -   2 Sets of 5 Reps (1-Arm Rotation)  
  -   2 Sets of 30-Second (One-Arm Hang, Pronated)  
  -   2 Sets of 30 Second (2 Bent-Arm Towel Swing)  
  -   2 Sets of 10 Reps (One-Arm Towel Switch)  
  -   Fav. Exercise

## Dynamic Forearm Training
Lighter weights + more sets = Bigger
Heavier weights = Stronger hands/forearms
